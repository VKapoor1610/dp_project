About Write-O-Braille

This project aims to establish a model that can teach primary students the fundamentals of braille, enabling them to achieve literacy without external assistance.

By providing visually impaired children with the tools and resources to learn braille on their own, this project seeks to empower them to overcome the barriers to education that they face.
